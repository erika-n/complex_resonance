Driven Harmonic Oscillator

  • Simple oscillator (blue) drives second oscillator (green)
  • Force 1 from simple harmonic oscillator
  • Force 2 = Force1 - spring_constant*position - damping_constant*velocity (Source)
  • Second oscillator synchronizes to frequency of first oscillator
  • Amplitude is maxiumum when natural frequency of second oscillator == 1st
