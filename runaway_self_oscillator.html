Runaway Self-oscillator

  • Simplest form of feedback oscillator
  • Drives itself (green) with a delayed version of its own position (blue)
  • Amplitude increases exponentially
  • Equivalent to negative damping
  • In real life: "The amplitude of the oscillation grows exponentially with time, until it becomes so large that nonlinear effects become relevant and somehow determine a limiting amplitude." Jenkins, Self-oscillation, pg. 8
