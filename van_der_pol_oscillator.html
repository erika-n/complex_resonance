Relaxation (Van der Pol) Oscillator

  • Oscillator with non-linear damping
  • Adds force proportional to the square of the position times the velocity
  • Force = Damped Oscillator Force (with negative damping constant) - constant*(position**2)*velocity (Source pg. 17)
  • Jagged wave with fast turns
  • "Incorporates the rapid nonlinear switching of a relaxation oscillator into the solution to the equation of motion."
    Self-oscillation, Jenkins, 2012, pg. 19
